About RouteWise

RouteWise is a smart road trip planning website that helps users plan their journey easily. Users can search their destination, view the travel route, check distance and travel time, and discover nearby hotels, restaurants, petrol pumps and EV charging stations. The main objective of this project is to provide all essential travel information on a single platform.

Our Mission

To simplify road trip planning using maps and location-based services.

Our Vision

To become a reliable travel companion for every road traveler.
